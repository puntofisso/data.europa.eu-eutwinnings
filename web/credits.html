This website uses data from Eurostat based on the Nomenclature of Territorial Units for Statistics (NUTS) 2021. The following datasets have been used:

  • Population on 1 January by broad age group, sex and NUTS 3 region (demo_r_pjanaggr3)
  • Population on 1 January by age, sex and NUTS 2 region (demo_r_d2jan)
  • Population density by NUTS 3 region (demo_r_d3dens)
  • Fertility indicators by NUTS 3 region (demo_r_find3)
  • Population change - Demographic balance and crude rates at regional level (NUTS 3) (demo_r_gind3)
  • Population structure indicators by NUTS 3 region (demo_r_pjanaggr3)
  • Gross domestic product (GDP) at current market prices by NUTS 3 regions (nama_10r_3gdp)
  • Gross value added at basic prices by NUTS 3 regions (nama_10r_3gva)
